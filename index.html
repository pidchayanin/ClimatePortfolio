Post-Program Action Plan

The immediate crisis of climate change, as seen in my hometown's flooding, underscores the urgent need to integrate climate consciousness into Asia's economic framework. My post-program action plan is focused on translating the policy knowledge and cross-cultural advocacy skills gained at Climate Action Week (CAW) into actionable, scalable initiatives within the startup and community spheres.

1. Institutionalizing Green Entrepreneurship via ELKXA:

I will immediately apply CAW's governance and sustainability metrics insights (complemented by my KAIST energy research) to my AI market intelligence platform, ELKXA. By integrating validated ESG and climate-risk metrics into the market analysis tools we provide, I will guide thousands of emerging startups in Korea and Southeast Asia to view "green" business models not just as ethical choices, but as the only profitable pathway for long-term growth.

2. Building a Climate Tech Hub (House of Founders):

I will leverage the global network and specialized knowledge acquired at CAW to establish the "House of Founders" co-living project as a nexus for sustainable innovation. I plan to organize recurring "Climate Tech Roundtables," making UN SDGs a non-negotiable standard for the foreign entrepreneurs we host. This direct influence will foster cross-border sustainable project development, building upon my experience in international collaboration from the ASPIRE Forum.

3. Grassroots Policy Translation and Dissemination:

Utilizing the positive citizen leadership skills honed through the UPG program and my existing community networks (TSAK, TKET), I will translate high-level CAW dialogue into localized action plans. I will host dedicated sessions (similar to the trash management initiative) for Korean and Thai professionals and students, advocating for climate-resilient practices and entrepreneurial advocacy at the municipal and regional levels.

Ultimately, my goal is to serve as a conduit: transforming the essential knowledge from CAW 2026 into the sustainable DNA of tomorrow's Asian unicorns and driving tangible environmental benefits across our shared communities.
